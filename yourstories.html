Lemon Chicken Extravaganza

Hello, I'm Alex! My favorite recipe is my grandma's lemon chicken. The zesty flavor of lemon combined with the tender, juicy chicken is simply unbeatable. The golden brown color and the irresistible aroma make it a family favorite. Read more...

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 lemons (juiced and zested)

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in lemon juice, zest, and olive oil.
  2. Bake until the chicken is cooked through and golden.
  3. Serve with your favorite side dishes.
