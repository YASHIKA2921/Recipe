Strawberry Milkshake

A refreshing and fruity milkshake made with fresh strawberries, milk, and a scoop of vanilla ice cream. Perfect for a hot day!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream

Instructions

  1. Wash and hull the strawberries.
  2. In a blender, combine strawberries, milk, and vanilla ice cream.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.

Pasta

A classic pasta dish with your choice of sauce, whether it's marinara, Alfredo, or pesto. Comfort food at its finest!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pasta
  • 1 cup pasta sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat the pasta sauce in a separate pan.
  3. Combine the cooked pasta with the sauce and toss until well coated.
